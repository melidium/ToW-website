Boss 1: Tricia

Tricia will cast vines that tangles you to death after 10 seconds (no matter how much HP you have). This can be easily dodged by watching out for 2 green circles on the floor. You can hit the vines to help set free anyone who's entangled.

Boss 2: Kode and Ezio

Everyone will have to separate into 2 groups to kill Ezio AND Kode at the same time. Be aware of their HP bars at all times, making sure both bosses are killed at the same speed. Killing Ezio or Kode first will make one of them go berserk (spamming full screen AOEs).
